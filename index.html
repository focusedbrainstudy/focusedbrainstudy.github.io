About Technology

During training we notice how our brain slowly starts to fade, we lose our attention to the workout, and the only thought is how great it would be to just stop

Experienced sportsman are more used to such behaviour, so they push themselves beyond expected limits, resulting in natural modulation of brain activity

I want to introduce a wearable tactile stimulating device, which would enhance control over an exercise by inducing responsiveness to the weight, and reduce an early exhaustion for best performance
